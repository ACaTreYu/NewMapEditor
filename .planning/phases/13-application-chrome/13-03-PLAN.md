14. Verify diagonal resize grip is visible in the bottom-right corner of the status bar

**Panel Dividers (CHROME-03):**
15. Verify the vertical divider (between canvas and animations) is a thin raised bar (~4px)
16. Verify the horizontal divider (between canvas and tiles) is a thin raised bar (~4px)
17. Hover over each divider -- verify only cursor changes (no background color change)
18. Drag each divider -- verify no highlight appears during drag

**Title Bars (CHROME-04):**
19. Click the animation panel -- verify its title bar shows a blue-to-lighter-blue gradient
20. Click the canvas area -- verify the animation panel title bar changes to grey gradient
